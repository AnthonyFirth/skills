# Minecraft Speedrunning Basics

Speedrunning Minecraft means beating the game — defeating the Ender Dragon — as fast as possible from a fresh world. The most popular category is **Any% Random Seed Glitchless (RSG) 1.16+**, where the seed is random and major glitches are banned.

## The Core Route

1. **Spawn & Nether prep** — Gather a few logs, craft a wooden pickaxe, mine stone, then find iron (often from village chests, shipwrecks, or surface caves). Build a Nether portal using either a lava pool + water bucket or 10 obsidian.
2. **Enter the Nether** — The goal here is to find a **Nether fortress** (for blaze rods) and **bartering gold** (for ender pearls from piglins).
3. **Blaze rods** — Kill blazes until you have at least 6–7 blaze rods. Craft them into blaze powder.
4. **Ender pearls** — Trade gold ingots to piglins until you have ~12+ ender pearls. Gold comes from bastions, nether gold ore, or trading.
5. **Stronghold triangulation** — Return to the Overworld, throw an Eye of Ender, note the angle, travel ~500+ blocks perpendicular, throw another, and triangulate the stronghold location.
6. **End portal** — Fill the portal frame with Eyes of Ender and jump in.
7. **Kill the dragon** — Destroy the End Crystals on the obsidian pillars (bed-bombing the dragon's head while it perches is the fastest finisher).

## Key Techniques

- **Bastion vs. fortress first** — Bastion routes (treasure or housing) can give massive gold + pearls quickly but are risky. Fortress-first is safer but slower on average.
- **Piglin bartering** — Wear gold boots to avoid aggro. Drop gold ingots one at a time to keep RNG rolls independent.
- **Blaze killing** — Stand under the spawner platform and hit blazes through the floor for safe kills.
- **Eye throws** — Eyes point toward the stronghold from the player's eye level. Use F3 to read facing angle precisely; two throws from different positions pinpoint the location.
- **Bed strats** — In the End, place a bed near the dragon's perch and detonate it for ~165 damage per bed. Bring 5–6 beds.

## Tools & Resources

- **Practice maps** — Tools like *MCSR Ranked* let you practice specific segments (Nether, blaze, stronghold).
- **Mod pack** — The community-approved *MCSR mod pack* (Sodium, Lithium, SpeedrunIGT, etc.) is allowed and provides the in-game timer.
- **Leaderboards** — Submissions go to [speedrun.com/mc](https://www.speedrun.com/mc). Verification requires video proof.

## Common Pitfalls

- Burning your bed in the Nether (don't sleep there).
- Forgetting to bring water for fire/lava in the End.
- Running out of pearls before triangulating — always barter for a buffer.
- Mis-reading the F3 angle on eye throws and overshooting the stronghold.

## Categories at a Glance

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| Any% RSG | Random seed, glitchless — the flagship category |
| Any% SSG | Set seed, glitchless — execution-focused |
| Any% Glitched | All glitches allowed, including world-corruption tricks |
| All Advancements | Complete every advancement |

World record pace for RSG 1.16 sits around **6–7 minutes**; sub-10 is considered a strong run for newer runners.
